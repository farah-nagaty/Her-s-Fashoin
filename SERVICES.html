OUR SERVICES

Order Tracking

1. Click the drop-down arrow beside your name and select Orders 2. Click SEE DETAILS in front of the order you want to track. 3. Click TRACK MY ITEM button in front of the order you wish to track 4. Delive ry history is displayed

Delivery

On average, it takes 8-10 business days with the Economy Service, 4-6 business days with Standard Service and 2-3 business days with Express Service

Return & Exchange

You have 14 days and up to 30 days for defective products to make a return request after your order has been delivered.

Store Finder

Enter your location to find a nearby store.

Order Cancellation

1. From the list of Orders in your Jumia Account, Click See Details in front of the order you wish to cancel. 2. Click the Cancel Item button. 3. Select the right quantity and cancellation reason from the drop-down list, and submit.
